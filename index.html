Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a solar installation take?+
Most residential installations are completed in 1–2 days. Commercial projects may take 3–5 days depending on size. We handle all permits so you don't have to.
How much can I save on my electricity bill?+
The average Houston homeowner saves 70–90% on their electricity bill. Your exact savings depend on your usage, system size, and roof orientation.
What is the Federal Solar Tax Credit?+
The federal government offers a 30% tax credit on the full cost of your solar installation. This is a dollar-for-dollar reduction in your federal income tax, not just a deduction.
Do solar panels work during cloudy days?+
Yes! Solar panels still generate electricity on cloudy days, though at reduced efficiency (10–25%). They also work in rain, just at lower output. Houston averages 204 sunny days per year.
What warranty do you offer?+
We offer a 25-year performance warranty on all panels, a 10-year inverter warranty, and a 5-year workmanship warranty on installation. We're fully licensed and insured in Texas.
Do you offer financing?+
Yes! We offer $0 down solar loans, leases, and power purchase agreements (PPAs). Many customers start saving from day one with zero upfront cost.
